About

Who we are.

A founder-led development company built from the ground up — in the most literal sense.

Background

Built from construction experience.

ROG Developments was founded by people who started in the construction industry and learned the business from the ground up. Before acquiring and developing sites, we spent years on tools, managing trades, delivering projects and understanding how buildings actually come together.

That experience informs everything we do. We understand construction costs, project timelines and site conditions in a way that a purely financial developer cannot. When we put a number on a feasibility, it reflects reality.

What We're Building

Disciplined development, one project at a time.

We are in an early stage of building a development business. That means we are selective about what we acquire, careful about how we structure deals, and clear-eyed about the risks involved in any project.

We are not trying to build a large portfolio quickly. We are focused on executing well on each project, building relationships with reliable partners and compounding what we learn.

01

Selective Acquisition

We only pursue sites where the planning fundamentals, construction feasibility and financial returns are all clearly sound before committing.

02

Reliable Partners

Financiers, planners, builders and agents. We invest in relationships that are built on transparency and follow-through.

03

Compounding Knowledge

Every project teaches us something. We carry those lessons forward and systematically improve our process with each new development.

Long-Term Perspective

We're interested in how this industry changes.

The construction industry has been slow to change. That's starting to shift. We have a genuine interest in how better systems, improved processes and emerging technology may over time improve the way development and construction projects are planned and delivered.

This is not the core of our business today, and we don't position ourselves as a technology company. But it shapes how we think about the long term — and why we invest time in understanding what's changing in our industry.

Our core business is property development. Everything else is context.

How We Work With Partners

Straightforward to deal with.

We work with lenders, brokers, solicitors, planners, agents and consultants regularly. We try to make those relationships easy. That means being prepared, being honest about where a project is at, and not wasting people's time.

If you're considering working with us or want to understand a specific project better, the best starting point is a direct conversation.
