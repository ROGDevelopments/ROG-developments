What We Do

Residential development, done properly.

We identify, acquire and develop residential properties across Greater Sydney. Our focus is on duplex, dual occupancy and low-rise multi-dwelling opportunities where the planning fundamentals are sound and the numbers work.

Every project starts with a thorough feasibility — planning controls, comparable sales, construction costs and financing. We don't move forward unless the case is clear.

I.

Site Acquisition

We source sites on-market and off-market, underpinned by detailed planning analysis and financial modelling before any offer is made.

II.

Project Delivery

We manage projects from DA or CDC approval through to construction completion, drawing on direct construction industry experience.

III.

Capital Structure

We work with lenders, brokers and financial partners to structure projects conservatively and maintain clear reporting throughout.

How We Think

Disciplined. Long-term. Straightforward.

We believe good development comes down to a few basic things: knowing your numbers, understanding the planning environment, building with quality, and being honest with your partners and financiers.

We are not trying to scale fast or build a large portfolio overnight. We are building a track record — project by project — and improving our process as we go.

We take the view that knowledge compounds. Every project teaches us something, and we try to carry those lessons forward systematically.

01

Know Your Numbers

Every site is modelled conservatively before we commit. Feasibility is not a formality — it is the foundation of every decision we make.

02

Understand the Planning Environment

NSW planning controls change. We stay close to what is possible — Housing SEPP, CDC pathways, LMR reforms — and factor it into every acquisition.

03

Be Honest With Partners

We maintain clear, straightforward communication with every financier, consultant and partner throughout the life of a project.

Current Focus

Greater Sydney — residential development.

Our current acquisition activity is focused on residential sites across Greater Sydney's established and growth suburbs. We are actively looking at sites suited to duplex and multi-dwelling development under NSW planning controls, including opportunities unlocked by recent Housing SEPP reforms.

We are also in the process of completing site assessments on a small number of active opportunities.
